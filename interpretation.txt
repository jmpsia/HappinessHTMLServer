* gdp (GDP per capita) - statistic  in purchasing power parity 
    at constant 2011 international dollar prices from 
    september 2017 update of the World Development Indicators (WDI)


* Life Expectancy: use WDI's non-health adjusted life expectancy as basis
    for calculation. Intrapolation and extrapolation is used to fill in mising values


* Social Support: national average of binary responses to question,
    "if you were in trouble, do you have relatives or friends you can count on
    to help you whenver you need them, or not?"


* Freedom to make life choices: "are you satisfied or dissatisfied
    with your freedom to choose what you do with your life?


* Generosity: residual of regressing national average of response to questions
    "have you donated money to a charity in the past month?" on GDP per capita


* Corruption Perception: national average of responses to two questions in GWP
    "is corruption widespread throughout the government or not"
    "is corruption widespread within businesses or not"
